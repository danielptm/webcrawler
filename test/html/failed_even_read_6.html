Events Calendar

Stockholm's stages and arenas attract Swedish and international stars, and large and small events come one after the next.
13 Jan

Japan - tales of objects and images

January 13 - April 30

Experience all facets of Japan! The exhibition shows over 400 objects from the urban communities of Japan - the Samurai, merchants and craftsmen. Most of the objects date from the Edo period (1615 - 1868) and include works of art through which we are given a glimpse of the entertainment, religious and tea ceremony performances in Japan and its contacts with the outside world.
